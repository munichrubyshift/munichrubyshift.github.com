Talks

Nikolay Sturm, Cucumber and RSpec for acceptance testing

After a first career in systems administration, I switched to rails development on www.helpster.de last summer. For a decade now, I am active in the open-source community, first as committer to the OpenBSD project and later building one of the first testing tools for puppet, cucumber-puppet.

In this talk I will describe our current process of feature specification at Helpster, focussing specifically on the usage of cucumber and rspec for recording acceptance criteria and creating a living documentation.

Alex P, Metrics, teh second attempt

After trying once to speak on that subject and failing, I don't really want to give up. So here we go, I'm going to speak about how's and why's of system monitoring. And monitoring in general. And about how to build your own monitoring tools.




Community
